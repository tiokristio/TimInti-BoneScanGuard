Background

Bone Damage Detection and Counting System

Image Processing Application for Bone Fracture Detection with YOLO Method Integrated with a Website aims to develop a tool for automatic detection and quantification of bone damage. Utilizing YOLO (You Only Look Once), a computer vision method, this project employs convolutional neural network (CNN) architecture to efficiently identify fractures and cracks in bones, reducing the potential for human error in medical diagnoses. By integrating this tool into medical practice, it is expected to enhance accuracy and response to cases of bone injuries, thus mitigating the impact of accidents and trauma.
